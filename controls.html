Table Controls

Moving the table around

Zoom: Wheel, +/-, R/F, Shift + W/S
Rotate: Shift + Mouse Wheel, Q/E, Shift + A/D, Shift + Up/Down
Pan: Drag, W/A/S/D, Arrow Keys
Recenter: Escape, 0
Go To View: 1-9
Set View Shortcut: Shift + 1-9

Selecting pieces

Select and Bring to Top: Click
Select in Place: Right Click
Select Multiple: Right Click or Shift + Click on Table and Drag
Add to Selection: Shift + Click or Drag Selection Window

When pieces are selected

Rotate: Shift + Mouse Wheel, Shift + A/D, Shift + Left/Right
Flip: Double Click, Space (works with mouse hovering and no selection)
Move: Drag
Collect / Stack: C
Xpand / Expose: X
Zcramble / Roll / RandomiZe: Z
